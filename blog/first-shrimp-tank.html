Starting Your First Shrimp Tank

Jan 12, 2025 · Beginner

Setting up your first shrimp tank can feel overwhelming, but with the right approach it’s simple and rewarding.

Tank Size

We recommend starting with at least 20–30 litres to ensure stable water parameters.

Substrate

Active soils help buffer pH for Caridina shrimp, while inert substrates are better for Neocaridina.

Final Thoughts

Patience is the key to shrimp success. Cycle your tank properly and avoid rushing livestock additions.
